"The most interesting briefing we've had so far."

What landed:

What didn't:
- Email section gave no confidence it actually read through the emails. Only 2 from 44, both junk. "I need more comprehensive feedback to give me confidence that it's really gone through it."

- Vault tasks include stale items — "Reconsider Angular for Spoons" is priority 9 but Spoons isn't active, LocalShout is the full focus. "Get Gmail AI up" might already be done.
- Not confident these are truly the top tasks. 44 items scored priority 9 — no differentiation at the top.
- Hourly Telegram status messages have noise: `digest: 6 emails` (meaningless), `gateway: skipped (sandbox)` and `model: skipped (sandbox)` (irrelevant checks that should be suppressed, not reported).
- No Jimbo activity on the blog or dashboard in 24+ hours.

## What we learned

1. **The vault tasks section is the breakout success.** First time it's worked well. The pre-scoring from `prioritise-tasks.py` is paying off, and Sonnet adds genuine editorial voice. But 528 active tasks with 44 at priority 9 means the top is too crowded — scores are running against stale PRIORITIES.md (last updated 2026-02-19, 13 days ago).

2. **The email pipeline is doubly starved.** Only 44 emails fetched (should be 100-150) AND the triage worker has no priorities/goals context (audit C1), so it can't tell what's relevant. Result: near-empty email section that provides no value.

3. **Example training is the highest-leverage next move.** Marvin is willing to do manual email triage and explain his reasoning. This directly addresses audit C2 (no examples anywhere in the pipeline). Strategy: `context/EMAIL_EXAMPLES.md` that workers read as context, editable over time.

4. **"Balance" is the meta-priority.** LocalShout is the overwhelming focus, but not at the expense of everything else. The briefing's job is to help Marvin find this balance — surface what matters across all of life, not just dev work.

5. **Experiment tracker is silent.** No runs logged in last 3 days for any task type. Either logging isn't firing (sift-digest Step 5 not executing) or wrong DB path. Without this, we can't track quality trends. The name "experiment-tracker" is also opaque — "pipeline-log" or "briefing-log" would be clearer.

6. **Jimbo isn't active outside briefings.** No blog posts, no dashboard activity in 24h. The accountability loop (20:00 check) should be catching this.
