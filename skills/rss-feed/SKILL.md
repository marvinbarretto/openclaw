# RSS Feed

The blog RSS feed is auto-generated by Astro from the content collection. No manual maintenance required.

## Feed details

- **URL:** `https://jimbo.pages.dev/rss.xml`
- **Source:** `/workspace/blog-src/src/pages/rss.xml.js`
- **Updates:** Automatically whenever a new post is committed and Cloudflare rebuilds

## How it works

The `@astrojs/rss` package generates the feed from all non-draft posts in the content collection. Every post's title, date, description, and link are included automatically.

When you publish a new post (see `blog-publisher` skill), the RSS feed updates on the next build — no extra steps needed.

## Linking

The feed is linked in the `<head>` of every page via the BaseLayout:

```html
<link rel="alternate" type="application/rss+xml" title="Jimbo's Blog" href="/rss.xml">
```
