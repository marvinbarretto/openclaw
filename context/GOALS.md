# Goals

Longer-term ambitions that inform what's worth paying attention to. Review monthly.

*Last updated: 2026-02-19*

## Build & Ship Products

I want to launch real products that people use. LocalShout and Spoons are the current focus. Product Hunt, indie dev communities, and developer tools are all relevant to this goal.

## Get Financially Organised

YNAB, budgeting, understanding where money goes. This is a current push — anything that helps with financial literacy or tools is welcome.

## Travel More, Spend Less

I love travelling and I'm good at finding deals. Error fares, creative routing, all-inclusive bargains, campervan relocations — I want to be the first to know about opportunities. Short-notice is fine.

## Stay Culturally Active

Gigs, comedy, cinema, meetups, debates, hiking. I want a full calendar of interesting things to do. Small and niche beats big and commercial. Knowing early is everything — events sell out.

## Keep Learning

Tech moves fast. I want to stay current on frontend, AI/agents, and developer tools without drowning in newsletters. Quality over quantity. The occasional deep read when I have time.

## Build a Network

Meetups, events, LinkedIn (passively). I'm not actively job hunting but I want to know what's out there and stay connected to the industry.

## Health & Wellbeing

Background goal. Nutrition, fitness, mental health, stoicism. Not a daily priority but I appreciate nudges.
